# Author Spec

Single source: the `/author-spec` slash-command
(`.claude/commands/author-spec.md`) is the invocation surface —
including the refuse-unless pre-conditions (IDEA at
`status: ready-for-spec`) and the owner-only `approved` rule; the
`spec-authoring` skill (`agents/skills/spec-authoring/SKILL.md`) owns
the mechanics. Shared schema: `agents/specs/SPEC.schema.md`.
